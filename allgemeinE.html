Common Activities

Presentations

For all participants the following presentations will be offered:

  • Geology of the Swabian Alb
  • cave genesis
  • materials needed for exploration
  • cave rescue
  • cave protection
  • bat protection
  • and much more...

Exkursionen

  • Field trip: There will be a geological excursion on the schäbische Alb (Swabian Jura).
  • Hike: We will hike in the Schmiech-, Ach- und Blau- valley and visit prehistoric sites.

The evenings at the camp are mainly for sociality and coming together. If you own a musical instrument or sports equipment, please bring it to the camp.
