The camp is located in the building of the caving club "Höhlenverein Blaubeuren" near Blaubeuren-Seissen. We can offer a meeting room, restrooms, shower and a campfire place for comfortable evenings. You can camp at the edge of the forest around the building. There is also enough place for motor homes. Electricity will be available only occasionally.

Even in summer it may be chilly in the evening and during bad weather. Please bring enough !warm colthes!

Image: introductions into materials needed for cave exploration.

Please bring the following basic equipment for caving:

Overall (Schlaz), enough warm underwear, e.g. under-schlaz, warm track suit or equivalent, rubber boots with a good profile, helmet with electrical lamp (no carbide lamp), spare lamp, accumulator loader.

The participation fee is:

Age 25 and younger260.- €(Sponsored by the EU)
Age 26 and older350.- €

This includes:

Image: the field kitchen.

For 16 days: Accommodation (Recreation room, place for a tent, restrooms with showers), 3 meals per day, support and training in small groups by experienced cavers, excursions and entrance fees for common activities, a CD with the course documentation, youth group insurance (health, third party liability, accident) for participants age 25 and younger for the duration of the camp. Please bring an international health insurance certificate to make processing easier.

For participants age 26 and older we strongly recommend to have their own insurance (health, third party liability, accident including salvage costs as offered for example for "Deutscher Alpenverein DAV"-members).
